    Microblading

    The au natural look.

    Microblading is only semi-permanent.

    • Microblading works by placing pigment into the skin.
    • The pigment isn't as deep as a tattoo, which is what makes it semi-permanent.
    • "

    Different to powder/ombre brow.

    • Microblading works with the eyebrows to make them look fuller.
    • Microblading is the more popular choice as it's a much more natural look.
    • "

    Slightly painful procedure.

    • As the procedure works with needles going into the skin, it's naturally going to sting.
    • You save on precious time in the morning though, whether you use that time for more sleep or to get more.
    • So it's short term vs long term.
    • "

    Can be altered

    • Every treatment requires a touch-up session.
    • This will give you the chance to have something altered.
    • So if you come to find they're too dark, that won't be a problem.
    • "

    Usually lasts between 9-15 months

    • This can be a comfort for alot of people.
    • Most chose to get it done again however as they're very pleased with not only the look but that precious time saved.
    • "
